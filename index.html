About

Sai is a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, Santa Cruz, where he conducts research under the supervision of Dr. Magy Seif El-Nasr in the GUII Lab. His research focuses on understanding human interactions with multimedia and technology. He particularly works on applications of graph theory and algorithms in the intersection of culture and video games. Sai has previously gained industry experience through internships at companies such as Meta (Facebook) and Invento Robotics. He has also served as a Research Scholar at both Georgia Tech and at the Indian Institute of Science. He possesses proficiency in both qualitative and quantitative research methods and is a skilled programmer. Additionally, he is an avid follower of cricket and Indian cinema, and likes to write about himself in the third person.
