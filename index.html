Sai Siddartha Maram

Sai is an first-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, Santa Cruz. He will be under the supervision of Dr.Magy. Sai's research revolves around understanding how Humans interact and visualize Multimedia and Technology. He works on Computer Vision, DL and Information Retrival techniques to mine interaction patterns in Gaming, Sports, Robotics and other verticals. He is an ardent follower of Cricket and Indian Cinema and likes to write about himself in 3rd person.
