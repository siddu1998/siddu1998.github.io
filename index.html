PhD Candidate at GUII lab, UC Santa Cruz. Previously at Meta, Xbox/Microsoft, Invento Robotics.

Crafting intuitive experiences, solving complex problems.

My approach to UX research is defined by a decade of navigating the intersection of AI, software, and human behavior. From engineering conversational robots at Invento Robotics to analyzing digital community dynamics at Meta, I’ve specialized in how emerging technologies integrate into the fabric of everyday life. Currently finishing my Ph.D. in HCI, my work focuses on building "grounded" AI—designing educational chatbots and collaborative tools that move beyond utility to foster genuine reflection and learning. Whether I’m investigating family gaming dynamics or building systems for collaborative inquiry, I aim to create technology that feels less like a tool and more like a thoughtful partner.
