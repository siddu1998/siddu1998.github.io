Hi, I am Sai Maram.

Facebook Sports Cards, Meta Notes-Spotify Connect were outcomes of my internship at Meta. I am also the researcher/designer/developer behind Xbox AURA. My work also contributed to award winning videogames like Halo and Perfect Dark.

My work explores how design, AI, and community come together to support the development of intuitive experiences. I have previously worked with Meta, Microsoft, and Xbox. If you'd like to connect or collaborate, feel free to reach out at msaisiddartha1@gmail.com
