Tales of Dharma

Game Design 2025 Card Game Over 1000 Copies Sold

Overview

Tales of Dharma is a card game designed to foster storytelling, family time, reflection, and community engagement through Hindu mythology. The game brings ancient narratives to life through beautifully illustrated cards featuring deities, asuras, and mythological characters.

With over 1000 copies sold, Tales of Dharma has successfully created a bridge between traditional cultural storytelling and modern gameplay, making mythology accessible and engaging for families and communities.

The Cards

Each card features unique character art and gameplay mechanics inspired by mythological narratives. Hover over the cards to see the 3D parallax effect.

Impact

Tales of Dharma has resonated with families and communities seeking meaningful ways to engage with cultural narratives. The game serves multiple purposes:

  • Family Bonding: Creates shared experiences around storytelling
  • Cultural Education: Introduces younger generations to mythological narratives
  • Community Engagement: Facilitates group discussions and reflections
  • Accessible Learning: Makes complex narratives approachable through gameplay

The success of over 1000 copies sold demonstrates the demand for culturally-grounded gaming experiences that bring families together.
