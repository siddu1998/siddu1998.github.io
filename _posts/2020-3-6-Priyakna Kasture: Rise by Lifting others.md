title: Priyanka Kasture : Rise by Lifting others
---

These 23 years of my life, my biggest learning has been to - keep doing good to others, keep sharing all that you have with others, always, even if that demands you to go a lot out of your way. Strive to be a star in someone's dark sky. Maybe, in the beginning, it'll be unappreciated, unreciprocated, or ignored, but some fine day, all the good that you passed on to others, will come back to you.


When I got started with Machine Learning India, I never in my wildest dreams had an idea of how big this little initiative of sharing valuable information was going to be. All I knew back then, was that I didn't want any passionate learner to go through the struggle of having no mentor, no guidance, no assistance, like I did. I wanted to share as much knowledge as I had, and more. My efforts went unnoticed for months together. There was only an incremental change in the number and the quality of people looking up to my content, with every week that was passing. But, I was giving everything I had, I was delivering, delivering with dedication, with consistency, with discipline, putting in more and more effort each day, learning and improving in the entire process. From - solving trivial doubts and helping my college juniors in their projects, to - staying up late at nights to respond to every email in the initial days, and help enthusiasts build their portfolios, I helped people in all big and small ways I could.


I kept doing good, without expecting returns of any kind. I did not refrain from grind. And you see where all this service, has gotten me today. Machine Learning India is a 1,23,000+ strong technical community at the moment, and is growing at an unimaginable pace. We have started conducting offline events of our own, under the community, and now are all set to go digital through webinars and podcasts from the month of May, to reach out to more people and take little steps towards our vision of democratizing AI and other cutting edge technologies.
What you should take away? Probably, two lines - always do good, and rise by lifting others.
