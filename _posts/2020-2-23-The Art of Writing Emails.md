Ever since the return from my internship at GeorgiaTech and acceptance as a research Intern at IISc Bangalore, I have had a lot of messages, emails, DM's asking one very important question "Hey, How did you land with an interview call in the first place?". Read along, to know how I had applied and what I learnt in the prcess which might help you.

Recently I recieved a message on Whatsapp from my junior, he wanted help in drafting an email to a professor he was intrested in working with. Here is the email.

Subject : Possible Undergraduate Research Opportunities

*Dear Dr.LMNOP,*

*I am ABC, studying 3rd year CSE undergraduate at XYZ university. I have a perfect cgpa of 10 and hold a merit scholarship. I have a great intrest in machine learning and completed courses from Andrew NG on coursera. I have noticed your labs work on machine learning based projects and wanted to request if you can provide an opportunity for me to intern in your lab during the summer of 2020. An internship under your guidence would act as a perfect platform for my future research ambitions.*

Lets break down the above email into its pro's and cons. Before we go any further these are purley based on my experiences and this discussion is not based on any ground truth or comprehensive set of rules. These are based on my personal intution and experiences.
